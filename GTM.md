# GTM

## Ideal user profile

Head of Engineering at a Series A startup (10-50 people, $500K-$2M ARR, 3-6 paid AI tools, limited visibility into per-tool ROI, often paying from shared cards and ad hoc procurement paths).

## Channels

1. Hacker News "Show HN" launch post targeting technical startup operators.
2. Reddit organic posts in r/SaaS and r/startups centered on practical savings outcomes.
3. Indie Hackers launch post with real screenshots and before/after spend examples.
4. Twitter/X replies and quote-tweets on posts complaining about AI tooling bills.
5. Cold outbound to YC W24/S24 CTOs with short personalized hooks.
6. Startup Slack communities (Ramen profitable, Product Hunt makers, niche founder groups).
7. Credex-owned distribution list as an unfair channel for immediate qualified traffic.

## Positioning

- "Free 2-minute AI spend audit"
- Financially defensible recommendations, not generic AI advice
- Practical next steps even for already-optimized teams
