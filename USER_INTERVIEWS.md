## Interview script prepared

1. What AI tools does your team currently pay for?
2. Who approves those expenses?
3. Do you know your current monthly AI tooling spend without checking invoices?
4. Have you ever removed or downgraded an AI tool because of overlap?
5. What evidence would make you trust an automated recommendation to switch, downgrade, or consolidate a tool?
6. Would a benchmark like "AI spend per developer" help you justify a budget decision?
7. When would you want Credex or another credit provider involved?
